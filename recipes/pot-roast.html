Slow Cooker Pot Roast

Description

This slow cooker pot roast recipe always turns out moist and juicy. It's cooked with carrots, onion, celery, and potatoes.

Ingredients

  • 4 pounds chuck roast
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 packet dry onion soup mix
  • 1 cup water
  • 3 carrots, chopped
  • 3 potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 stalk celery, chopped

Steps

  1. Season roast generously with salt and pepper.
  2. Heat oil in a large skillet over high heat; add roast and sear, about 4 minutes per side.
  3. Place roast in the slow cooker; sprinkle soup mix on top.
  4. Add water; scatter carrots, potatoes, onion, and celery on top.
  5. Cover and cook on Low for 8 to 10 hours.
